Rachel S. Khmelnitsky spent her undergrad focusing on the intellectual, political, and cultural developments between the United States and the Soviet Union as dominating Empires of the twentieth century.

This website is a section of Rachel’s interest within the Cold War cultural and intellectual history. Both main pages are collections of highlights of how capitalist and aspiring communist visions of future differ and relate and how have those imagining affected the curation of culture within their respective mid-century media and design creations.

This website is also mostly filled by background knowledge the author obtained from prior research and lectures, for certain topics there is a source section in the home and are linked within certain images.
